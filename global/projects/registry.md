### Status values

- **active** — Ongoing work or regular use.
- **paused** — On hold; may resume.
- **completed** — Delivered or sunset by intent.
- **archived** — Kept for reference only; no active work expected.

Add one row per project. Keep paths absolute or consistently relative to a documented root. Update **Last session** when a meaningful session ends or on a regular cadence if preferred.
